Mesh must be stored locally (i.e. on the C: Drive etc)
Alt + Shift to contrain rotation in texture viewport
Shift right click for light
m for material
c for solo map
Ctrl+Right or left click to control brush settings
F - Frame whole mesh
Click orbit around a spot on the mesh to pivot on that spot
F1-F3 for viewport options
Right-click on layer and Add Mask with color selection to use a color id from the baked id map
Alt click on mask to solo it
Ctrl + G to group layers
or right-click
Can copy and paste mask
Edit > Project config > Edit mesh to change mesh (but care for physical painting els emight lose brushstrokes)
